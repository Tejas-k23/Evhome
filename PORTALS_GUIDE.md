# EV Home - Portals Access Guide

This guide provides instructions on how to access the Admin and Station Owner portals for the EV Home platform.

## 🛡️ Admin Portal
The Admin Portal allows you to manage all users, charging stations, and view site-wide statistics.

- **URL**: [http://localhost:5173/admin123](http://localhost:5173/admin123)
- **Access Key**: `EVHOME@123`

### Features:
- Station Management (Add/Edit/Delete)
- User Management
- Booking Overviews
- Billing & Revenue tracking

---

## 🏠 Station Owner Portal
The Owner Portal is designed for individual station owners to manage their specific stations and view their revenue.

- **URL**: [http://localhost:5173/owner](http://localhost:5173/owner)
